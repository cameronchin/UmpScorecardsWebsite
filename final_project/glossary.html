Umpire

The home-plate umpire for that game

Game Date

The date that the game was played

Away Team

The home team (abbreviated -- see here for full team names)

Home Team

The home team (abbreviated -- see here for full team names)

Away Team Runs

The number of runs scored by the away team

Home Team Runs

The number of runs scored by the home team

Pitches Called

The total number of pitches called by the home-plate umpire

Correct Calls

The total number of correct calls made by the umpire, defined by UmpScorecards here

Incorrect Calls

The total number of incorrect calls made by the umpire, defined by UmpScorecards here

Accuracy

The percentage of correct calls made by the umpire

Consistency

The percentage of calls that are consistent with the strike zone established by the umpire during the game, defined by UmpScorecards here

Home Favor

The difference between the home team and away team's run expectancy impact by the umpire, defined by UmpScorecards here. A positive home favor means that the umpire's calls favored the home team overall, while a negative home favor means that the umpire's calls favored the away team overall

Total Run Impact

The cumulative run expectancy impact by the umpire (i.e. away favor + home favor)
